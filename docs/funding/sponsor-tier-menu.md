# Aligned Corporate Sponsorship — Tier Menu

**Status:** Draft v0.1 — for outreach to aligned-mission corporate sponsors
**Last updated:** 2026-05-10

This document is the public sponsorship tier menu for **mission-aligned
corporate sponsors** of OMNI OS. It is the operational counterpart to
[`docs/08-funding-policy.md`](../08-funding-policy.md) § "Accepted sources"
Tier 2.

## Eligibility

To be eligible as a sponsor, the company MUST:

1. **Not be on the Excluded Sources list** in
   [`docs/08-funding-policy.md`](../08-funding-policy.md). In particular: no
   FAANG, no telcos, no banks, no cloud hyperscalers, no advertising
   networks, no entities with regulatory authority over privacy/AI/telecom.
2. **Have a business model aligned** with the OMNI OS mission (privacy,
   user autonomy, open source).
3. **Pass conflict-of-interest review** per Stichting OMNI bylaws Article 8.

Examples of aligned companies (non-exhaustive):

- **Privacy-focused service providers**: Proton AG, Tutanota, Mullvad VPN,
  IVPN, Element / New Vector, Threema, Brave Software.
- **Privacy-focused hardware vendors**: System76, Framework, Purism,
  Nitrokey, Solokeys, MNT Reform.
- **Open-source-aligned organizations**: GitLab, GitHub (case-by-case
  given Microsoft ownership), JetBrains (independent foundations).

## What sponsors do NOT get

- **No influence over protocol direction.** OIPs are decided by federated
  vote; sponsors vote only if they operate TEE-attested mesh nodes, with
  vote weight equal to any individual node operator.
- **No preferential commercial license terms.** Commercial license pricing
  is published; sponsors pay the same as any other commercial licensee.
- **No board seats by virtue of sponsorship.** Trustees are appointed per
  bylaws Article 4; sponsorship does not create eligibility.
- **No access to user data.** OMNI OS's cryptographic guarantees apply to
  sponsors equally.
- **No exclusivity.** Multiple sponsors at the same tier are welcome.

## Tier amounts (annual)

| Tier | Annual fee (EUR) | Estimated coverage |
|---|---|---|
| Bronze | 5,000 | Infrastructure + community event budget for one quarter |
| Silver | 25,000 | One developer-week of engineering or one cryptographer-week |
| Gold | 100,000 | Approximately 4 months of one engineer FTE |
| Platinum | 500,000+ | Significant contribution to the operating budget; bespoke arrangements available |

Sponsorship is paid annually in advance. Recurring sponsors get a renewal
notification 90 days before expiry. Sponsorship is non-refundable but can
be terminated by either party at any time (forfeiting the unused portion).

## Anti-capture safeguards

Several structural safeguards prevent a sponsor from gaining
disproportionate influence:

1. **30% rule** (bylaws Article 9.1 + funding policy): no single funder
   exceeds 30% of operating budget after year 2. This forces diversification.
2. **No protocol influence** (above): governance is layer 2 (OIP), separated
   from funding (layer 3).
3. **Public acknowledgement only** (this document): sponsorship is publicly
   disclosed; covert sponsorship is rejected.
4. **Sponsor Roundtable is not a board.** Quarterly call exists for
   transparency and feedback; sponsors have no decision-making authority.
5. **Termination clauses:** if a sponsor's business model changes such that
   they fall onto the Excluded Sources list, sponsorship terminates
   immediately and remaining funds are returned (pro-rata).
6. **Founder conflict disclosure:** if any trustee or officer has a material
   financial interest in a sponsor, they recuse from any decision involving
   that sponsor.

## Application process

1. Sponsor reaches out via `hello@omni-os.org` (post-Stichting) or
   `cySalazar@cySalazar.com` (current).
2. Foundation Director (or founder, pre-Director) reviews eligibility
   against this document and bylaws.
3. Board approves sponsorship at next board meeting (typically within 90
   days). If approved, sponsorship agreement is signed.
4. Sponsor's logo and acknowledgement land on website and repository within
   30 days of payment receipt.

## Termination

Either party may terminate the sponsorship agreement with 90 days' notice.
The Foundation reserves the right to terminate immediately if:

- The sponsor falls onto the Excluded Sources list.
- The sponsor publicly takes a position incompatible with the OMNI OS
  Mission Anchor (bylaws Article 3).
- The sponsor demands governance influence beyond what this document
  describes.

Termination does not retroactively invalidate past attribution; the
sponsor's logo is removed from forward-looking materials only.

## Sponsorship as a hiring signal

The Foundation does NOT use sponsorship to filter hiring candidates. Employees
of sponsor companies are welcome to apply to OMNI OS positions but do not
receive preferential consideration.

## Updates

This tier menu is reviewed annually by the board. Amendments do not affect
existing sponsorship agreements until renewal; sponsors are notified at
least 90 days before any change takes effect.
